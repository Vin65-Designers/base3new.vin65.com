<v65:product:media></v65:product:media>

The v65:product:media tag displays product media uploaded for current product.

Available on:

  • Product QuickView
  • Product Drilldown
Data Sheet
The v65:product:media tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-media">
	<div class="v65-product-mediaTitle">Data Sheet</div>
	<div class="v65-product-mediaThumbnail"><a href="//link to media//"></a></div>
</div>
