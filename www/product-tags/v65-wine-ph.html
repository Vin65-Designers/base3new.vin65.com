The v65:wine:ph tag displays the wine PH value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
PH
31
The v65:wine:ph tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-ph">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">PH</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">31</div>
</div>
