<v65:product:photos></v65:product:photos>

The v65:product:photos tag displays product photos uploaded for current product.

Available on:

  • Product Detail
The v65:product:photos tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-photos">
	<ol>
		<li><img src="/assets/images/products/list/Wine-1.jpg"></li>
		<li><img src="/assets/images/products/list/Wine-2.jpg"></li>
		<li><img src="/assets/images/products/list/Wine-3.jpg"></li>
	</ol>
</div>
