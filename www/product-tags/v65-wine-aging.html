<v65:wine:aging></v65:wine:aging>

The v65:wine:aging tag displays the wine Aging value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Aging
American Oak 7 Months
The v65:wine:aging tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-aging">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Aging</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">American Oak 7 Months</div>
</div>
