The v65:product:teaser tag displays the products Teaser attribute

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
  • Product Drilldown

This is a product teaser typicaly it should entice people to click on the wine to learn more but still be short. Learn more about product teasers in the documentation.

The v65:product:teaser tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-teaser">
	<p>This is a product teaser typicaly it should entice people to click on the wine to learn more but still be short. Learn more about product teasers in the documentation.</p>
</div>
