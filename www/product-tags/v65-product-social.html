<v65:product:social></v65:product:social>

The v65:product:social tag displays the social share widget.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
  • Product Drilldown

The v65:product:social tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-social">
	<img src="/assets/images/social/facebook-share.png">
	<img src="/assets/images/social/twitter.png">
	<img src="/assets/images/social/pinterest.png">
	<img src="/assets/images/social/email.png"><br>
	<img src="/assets/images/social/facebook-like.png">
</div>
