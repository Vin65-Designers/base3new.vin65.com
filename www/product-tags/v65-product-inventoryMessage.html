<v65:product:inventoryMessage></v65:product:inventoryMessage>

The v65:product:inventoryMessage tag displays the inventory message if necessary.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
  • Product Drilldown
In Stock

or

There are only 24 left.

or

On Back Order

or

Our Of Stock
The v65:product:inventoryMessage tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-inventoryMessage">
	//Message from Admin Panel//
</div>


or


<div class="v65-product-inventoryMessage">
	<span class="v65-product-inventoryMessageCurrentInventory">There are only 24 left.</span>
</div>


or


<div class="v65-product-inventoryMessage">
	<span class="v65-product-inventoryMessageOnBackOrder">On Back Order</span>
</div>


or


<div class="v65-product-inventoryMessage">
	<span class="v65-product-inventoryMessageOutOfStock">Our Of Stock</span>
</div>
