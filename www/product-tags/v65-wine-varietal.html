<v65:wine:variatal></v65:wine:variatal>

The v65:wine:variatal tag displays the wine Varietal value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Varietal
Chardonnay
The v65:wine:variatal tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-varietal">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Varietal</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">Chardonnay</div>
</div>
