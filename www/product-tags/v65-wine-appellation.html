<v65:wine:appellation></v65:wine:appellation>

The v65:wine:appellation tag displays the wine Appellation value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Appellation
Paso Robles
The v65:wine:appellation tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-applellation">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Appellation</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">Paso Robles</div>
</div>
