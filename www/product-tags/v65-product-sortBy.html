<v65:product:sortBy></v65:product:sortBy>

The v65:product:sortBy tag displays product sortBy dropdown.

Available on:

  • Product List
Sort by
The v65:product:sortBy tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-productSortBy"> 
	<span class="v65-label"> 
		Sort by 
		<div class="v65-selectWrapper"> 
			<select name="sortby"> 
				<option> Price (low to high)</option> 
				<option> Price (high to low)</option> 
			</select> 
		</div> 
	</span> 
</div>
