<v65:wine:bottlingDate></v65:wine:bottlingDate>

The v65:wine:bottlingDate tag displays the wine Bottling Date value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Bottling Date
January 2005
The v65:wine:bottlingDate tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-bottlingDate">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Bottling Date</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">January 2005</div>
</div>
