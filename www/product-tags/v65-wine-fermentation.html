<v65:wine:fermentation></v65:wine:fermentation>

The v65:wine:fermentation tag displays the wine Fermentation value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Fermentation
12 months oak
The v65:wine:fermentation tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-fermentation">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Fermentation</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">12 months oak</div>
</div>
