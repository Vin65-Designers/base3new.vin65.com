<v65:wine:vintage></v65:wine:vintage>

The v65:wine:vintage tag displays the wine Vintage value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Vintage
2007
The v65:wine:vintage tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-attribute v65-wine-vintage">
	<div class="v65-wine-attributeLabel">Vintage</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-attributeValue">2007</div>
</div>
