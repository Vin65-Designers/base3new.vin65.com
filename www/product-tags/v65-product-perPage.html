<v65:product:perPage></v65:product:perPage>

The v65:product:perPage tag displays products per page dropdown.

Optional Attribute(s):

productCountList:   <v65:product:perPage productCountList="12,24,48,all"></v65product:perPage>

Available on:

  • Product List
Products per page
The v65:product:perPage tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-product-perPage">
	<span class="v65-label">
		Products per page 
		<div class="v65-selectWrapper">
			<select name="perPage">
				<option>12</option>
				<option>24</option>
				<option>48</option>
				<option>All</option>
			</select>
		</div>
	</span>
</div>
