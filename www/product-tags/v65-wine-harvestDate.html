<v65:wine:harvestDate></v65:wine:harvestDate>

The v65:wine:harvestDate tag displays the wine Harvest Date value.

Available on:

  • Product List
  • Product QuickView
Harvest Date
January 2005
The v65:wine:harvestDate tag outputs the following HTML code:
<div class="v65-wine-spec v65-wine-harvestDate">
	<div class="v65-wine-specLabel">Harvest Date</div>
	<div class="v65-wine-specValue">January 2005</div>
</div>
